About Our Internship

At Katmai Computing, we're revolutionizing quantum computing by creating hardware that fits in your hand. Our summer internship program offers college juniors and seniors the opportunity to work on cutting-edge technology from anywhere in the world.

Remote Work

Work from anywhere with an internet connection. No relocation necessary.

Duration

8-week intensive program starting Sunday, May 4th.

Eligibility

Open to college juniors and seniors with a passion for quantum computing.

Internship Tracks

We're accepting 10 talented students across two specialized tracks:

UI/UX Design (5 positions)

Create intuitive interfaces for quantum computing systems. You'll work on:

  • User research and testing
  • Interface design for complex systems
  • Creating prototypes
  • Collaborating with firmware engineers

Firmware Development (5 positions)

Build the software that powers our quantum computing hardware. You'll work on:

  • Low-level programming
  • Quantum algorithm implementation
  • Hardware-software integration
  • Performance optimization
